About The Game

Call of Duty is one of the most popular first-person shooter games in the world. Players take the role of soldiers fighting in modern warfare, historical battles, or futuristic combat zones. The game includes exciting campaign missions, competitive multiplayer battles, and the famous battle royale mode called Warzone.

Popular Call of Duty Games

Modern Warfare

A realistic war experience with tactical missions and intense multiplayer battles.

Warzone

A massive battle royale mode where 150 players fight to be the last squad alive.

Black Ops

Covert operations, secret missions, and the famous Zombies survival mode.
